## Source treatment
- Official specs/manpages/RFCs/manufacturer support pages: strong.
- Community forums/XDA/SamMobile/SamFW/iFixit/ManualsLib: useful but review-required.
- Retailers/marketplaces: only for model, part number, compatibility, component references; not accepted as primary firmware/error-code authority.
- Blogs/general media: weak auxiliary sources only.
