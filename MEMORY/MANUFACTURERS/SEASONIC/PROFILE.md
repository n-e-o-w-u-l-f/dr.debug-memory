## Limitations

Dr.Debug must not suggest opening a PSU, bypassing protections or performing unsafe mains/high-voltage tests. Product-specific claims require exact Seasonic model, wattage, platform/revision where known, cable family, regional warranty scope and document version. Manuals are source references only; no Seasonic manual, installer, firmware, utility or binary is downloaded or rehosted by this profile.
