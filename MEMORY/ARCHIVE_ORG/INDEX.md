## Read-before-work rule

Before archive work, read `/AGENTS.md`, `/MEMORY/INDEX.md`, this file, `/MEMORY/SOURCES/INDEX.md`, `/MEMORY/SOURCE_TRUST/INDEX.md` and proposal/status indexes.

## Policy summary

Official source first. Do not rehost drivers, firmware, BIOS, installers or vendor tools by default. Manuals/documentation may be preservation candidates when official sources disappeared or are unstable and metadata/takedown contact are documented.
